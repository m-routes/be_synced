Welcome to the Be Synced app, developed by M-routes. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with Google Play requirements and applicable privacy laws.

We may collect the following types of information:

3. Background Location Use

Be Synced collects location data even when the app is closed or not in use. This is required for features like route tracking and geofence-based alerts. You can disable location access from device settings at any time.
