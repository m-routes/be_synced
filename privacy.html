1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share your personal data when you use our mobile or web services (“Services”). By using Be Synced, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, role (Student, Driver, Admin), school name.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To register and manage your account
  • To track student transport routes and attendance
  • To provide real-time chat features

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on user consent, contractual necessity (for school transport services), and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

Your data is not sold. It may be shared only:

  • With school admins for attendance and transport management

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for school operational needs or legal compliance.

7. Your Rights

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including encryption and access controls.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be stored on secure servers outside your country (e.g., Firebase in the U.S.). We ensure protection through appropriate safeguards.

10. Cookies

We use cookies only for session management. No personal data is stored in cookies.
